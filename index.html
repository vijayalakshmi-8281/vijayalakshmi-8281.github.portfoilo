My Projects

Moisture Detection in Sanitary Pads

Moisture detection technology integrated into the sanitary pad works like a builtin sensor. It senses when the pad gets soaked with fluid. Once it hits a certain level of wetness, it sets off a signal, popping up a small indicator. This alert system makes it simple for both users and caregivers to know when it's time for a change. This technology isn't as complicated as it might sound. It's kind of like how your phone knows when it's been rained on and tells you with a notification. caregivers, especially those looking after individuals who can't communicate well or are bedridden, this feature is a game-changer. They don't have to keep checking or second-guessing if the pad needs changing. They can simply glance at the indicator and know instantly what needs to be done, ensuring proper hygiene and comfort for the person they're caring for.
